Capture Details:

You can capture Pokemon by pressing the 1x or 10x buttons. Each button will allow you to perform one or ten captures at a time, respectively. Each Pokemon is categorized by R, SR, and SSR rarity and the standard probability of drawing each type is as follows:

  • R: 85.00%
  • SR: 11.00%
  • SSR: 4.00%

    • The probability of capturing each individual Pokemon within a particular rarity is equally weighted. Performing a 10x pull will guarantee that at least one of the Pokemon captured will be of an SR rarity or higher. Performing a 1x pull ten times will not produce the same effect.
